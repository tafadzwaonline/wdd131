Zimbabwe Travel Guide

A planning document for a responsive travel information website focused on destination discovery, trip ideas, and practical planning for visitors to Zimbabwe.

Site Name

Zimbabwe Travel Guide

I chose this name because it clearly describes the subject of the website and makes the purpose easy to understand for first-time visitors. It fits a travel audience and supports a clean, informative brand identity.

Optional domain availability: zimbabwetravelguide.org

Site Purpose

The site will provide destination information about Zimbabwe for people who want to explore natural attractions, historical places, and travel tips before planning a trip. It will include destination highlights, practical planning guidance, and an interactive form that helps users choose travel ideas based on their interests.

Scenarios

  • Which places in Zimbabwe are best for wildlife, nature, or heritage travel?
  • What season is best for visiting different destinations in Zimbabwe?
  • How can I choose a destination that matches the kind of trip I want to take?
